The Origin

J-VOX didn't start in a tech lab — it started at a kitchen table. It was built by a father who watched his son with disabilities struggle to express the full complexity of his thoughts using standard, static communication boards.

The Technology

The world moves too fast for 20 static buttons. J-VOX bridges the gap between simple requests and true expression — using Groq AI to provide an adaptive, intelligent voice that grows with the user.

The Legacy

This is a legacy project. It was built for my son, but it is shared with the world because every person deserves to be heard.

What is J-VOX?

J-VOX is a full-featured AAC (Augmentative and Alternative Communication) app designed for non-verbal individuals and their families. It gives users a fast, expressive, and personal way to communicate — built around their life, their words, and their needs.

Core Features
📂
Category Boards
Organized phrase libraries across feelings, food, activities, social, and more — fully editable.
🔤
Word Builder
Build sentences symbol by symbol with a core vocabulary grid and scene-based word sets.
✨
Magic Wand
AI-powered sentence refinement that polishes your words before speaking.
✦
Aura Mode
Smart environmental awareness using camera, microphone, and GPS to suggest the right phrases at the right moment.
🌿
Regulate
Sensory and emotional regulation tools including Zen Mode and a personal audio matrix.
🧒
About Me
Personalized identity cards your child can share — name, family, emergency contacts, and more.
📱
Installable PWA
Works offline, installs to your home screen, and runs on any device.
How to Use
1Tap a category to open its phrase board, then tap any button to speak.
2Use Word Builder to compose longer sentences word by word, then tap Speak.
3Go to ⚙️ Settings → About Me to personalize the identity phrases with your child's name, age, and family info.
4Unlock Edit Mode to add, remove, or rearrange any button in any category.
5Toggle ✦ Aura at the top to activate smart environmental communication suggestions.
6Back up your data regularly using 💾 Backup My Data in Settings.
